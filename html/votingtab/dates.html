ALL REGISTERED ST LAWRENCE COUNTY VOTERS, REGARDLESS OF PARTY ENROLLMENT, ARE ELIGIBLE TO VOTE IN THAT ELECTION.

Early voting will occur at our canton board of elections office (at the human services building, 80 state highway 310, second floor) In the two weeks before each upcoming special, primary or general election
